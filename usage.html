Legal & Compliance

Acceptable Use Policy

Rules governing conduct on the Shamwari platform to ensure security, legality, and a positive community experience across all services.

Zero Tolerance Enforced Reportable
Section 01

Purpose & Scope

This Acceptable Use Policy governs your conduct on the Shamwari platform to ensure security, legality, and positive community experience.

Policy Intent

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your conduct on the Shamwari platform to ensure security, legality, and positive community experience across all services including Totem Exchange, Live Contracts, App Store, and BetaChains.

  • Applies to all users, developers, merchants, and node operators
  • Covers all Shamwari services and BetaChain networks
  • Complements the Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy
  • Violations may result in immediate enforcement action
Section 02

Prohibited Conduct

You agree NOT to engage in any of the following activities on the Shamwari platform.

2.1 Illegal Activities

  • Money laundering, terrorist financing, or sanctions violations
  • Fraud, phishing, or social engineering attacks
  • Trafficking in illegal goods or services via Commerce
  • Copyright or trademark infringement
  • Identity theft or impersonation
  • Ransomware distribution or extortion
  • Child exploitation or abuse material
  • Stalking or harassment of individuals

2.2 Security Violations

  • Attempting to break post-quantum cryptographic protections
  • Sybil attacks on BetaChain consensus mechanisms
  • DDoS attacks or resource exhaustion attempts
  • Reverse engineering platform code
  • Exploiting smart contract vulnerabilities
  • Unauthorized access to nodes or infrastructure
  • Man-in-the-middle or replay attacks
  • Circumventing rate limits or access controls

2.3 Platform Integrity Violations

  • Market manipulation on Totem Exchange (wash trading, spoofing, layering)
  • False or misleading Digital Certificate submissions
  • Submitting fraudulent Insurance claims
  • Distributing malicious code through the App Store
  • Circumventing Chain Control & Taxation reporting
  • Creating fake accounts or bot networks
  • Front-running or MEV extraction attacks
  • Oracle manipulation or data poisoning

2.4 Harassment & Abuse

  • Harassing other users, developers, or merchants
  • Impersonating Shamwari staff or partners
  • Distributing harmful or offensive content
  • Doxxing or exposing private information
  • Hate speech or discriminatory conduct
  • Spam or unsolicited bulk communications
Section 03

Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in a range of escalating enforcement measures.

Suspension

Immediate suspension of platform access for severe or ongoing violations. Trading, contract execution, and wallet functionality may be frozen pending investigation.

Permanent Ban

Permanent ban from all Shamwari services for egregious violations including fraud, market manipulation, or security attacks. Associated wallet addresses may be blacklisted.

Legal Action

Reporting to law enforcement and regulatory authorities as required by Chain Control & Taxation frameworks. Civil or criminal prosecution may follow.

Asset Freezing

Freezing of associated wallet functionalities and staked assets or rewards where applicable and legally permissible. Frozen assets may be subject to regulatory seizure orders.

Forfeiture

Forfeiture of staked assets, rewards, or incentives where violations directly involve those assets or where required by regulatory order.

Section 04

Reporting Violations

How to Report

Report suspected violations to our security team with relevant evidence and transaction hashes. All reports are treated confidentially.

  • Include transaction hashes and wallet addresses involved
  • Provide screenshots, logs, or other evidence
  • Describe the violation with timestamps
  • Anonymous reports are accepted but may limit investigation

Cooperation with Authorities

Shamwari cooperates with law enforcement and regulatory agencies as required by Chain Control & Taxation frameworks and applicable law.

  • Lawful data requests are processed per jurisdiction
  • Emergency disclosures for imminent harm
  • Regulatory reporting for suspicious activity
  • Court orders and subpoenas are complied with
