Privacy Policy

How Shamwari collects, uses, stores, and protects your information across our multi-jurisdictional blockchain ecosystem and BetaChain networks.

GDPR Aligned Multi-Jurisdiction Immutable Ledger Notice
Section 01

Introduction

Shamwari operates a multi-jurisdictional blockchain ecosystem. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information across all services.

Scope of Coverage

This policy applies to all Shamwari services including Live Contracts, the Shamwari App Store, Digital Certificates, Totem Exchange, Commerce, Insurance, and all BetaChain networks.

  • Platform accounts and wallet services
  • Smart contract interactions
  • Cross-chain transactions
  • Third-party app integrations

Effective Date & Updates

This policy is effective as of April 2026. We may update it to reflect changes in our practices or regulatory requirements. Material changes will be notified via platform announcements and email.

  • Minor updates: Posted with revised date
  • Material changes: 30-day advance notice
  • Blockchain immutability disclosures cannot be altered
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance
Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect several categories of data to operate the Shamwari platform securely and compliantly.

Account Information

Identity verification data (KYC/AML), contact details, government-issued ID for regulated services, and authentication credentials.

Blockchain Data

Wallet addresses, public keys, transaction hashes, smart contract interactions, and BetaChain node participation records.

Platform Usage

Live Contract logs, App Store activity, Commerce history, Insurance policies, and Totem Exchange trading data.

Immutable Ledger Disclosure

Blockchain transactions are recorded on immutable distributed ledgers. While wallet addresses are pseudonymous, transaction details are permanently visible on-chain. We cannot delete, modify, or erase data recorded on the Shamwari blockchain or BetaChains.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

Platform Operation

Facilitating transactions, executing Live Contracts, maintaining BetaChain consensus, and ensuring network stability across all jurisdictions.

Security & Fraud Prevention

Implementing post-quantum cryptographic protections, intrusion detection, anomaly monitoring, and safeguarding against unauthorized access.

Regulatory Compliance

Satisfying multi-jurisdiction taxation requirements, Chain Control obligations, AML/KYC mandates, and regulatory reporting across BetaChains.

Service Delivery

Processing Commerce payments, managing Insurance policies, enabling Totem Exchange order matching, and issuing Digital Certificates.

Platform Improvement

Analytics for platform optimization, user experience enhancement, and infrastructure scaling across the BetaChain network.

Section 04

Data Sharing & Transfers

BetaChain Multi-Jurisdiction

Your data may be processed across multiple jurisdictional BetaChains. Each BetaChain operates under specific regional regulations. By using Shamwari, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred between jurisdictions as required by blockchain consensus and operational necessity.

Third-Party Disclosures

  • Regulatory Authorities: When required by Chain Control & Taxation frameworks or legal obligation
  • Service Providers: Infrastructure, security auditing, and analytics partners under strict confidentiality
  • Smart Contract Counterparties: Necessary execution data for Live Contracts and Commerce
  • Insurance Partners: Relevant claim and policy data for underwriting

Section 05

Security & Your Rights

Data Security

We employ post-quantum cryptographic algorithms to protect data in transit and at rest. However, no system is entirely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your private keys and authentication credentials.

Data Retention

Off-chain personal data is retained as long as necessary for service provision, legal compliance, and fraud prevention. On-chain data persists indefinitely per blockchain architecture.

Your Privacy Rights

Access

Request a copy of your personal data held by Shamwari.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

Restriction

Request limitation of processing under certain conditions.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Objection

Object to certain processing activities, where applicable.

Blockchain Limitation

While we honor these rights for off-chain data, we cannot alter, delete, or remove data committed to the immutable Shamwari blockchain or BetaChains.

Section 06

International Transfers & Contact

International Data Transfers

Given our multi-jurisdiction BetaChain infrastructure, your data may be transferred globally. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place consistent with applicable data protection laws including GDPR adequacy decisions and Standard Contractual Clauses.

Children's Privacy

Shamwari is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately for deletion.

Questions About Your Privacy?
