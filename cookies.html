Section 01

What Are Cookies

Cookies and similar technologies help us provide, secure, and improve the Shamwari platform.

Definition & Scope

Cookies and similar technologies (local storage, pixel tags, session storage) help us provide, secure, and improve the Shamwari platform. This policy explains what we use and how you can control your preferences.

  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device by your browser
  • Local Storage: Persistent data stored in your browser for UI preferences
  • Pixel Tags: Tiny invisible images used for analytics and error tracking
  • Session Tokens: Cryptographic identifiers for authenticated sessions
Section 02

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Cannot be disabled without losing platform functionality. Essential for platform operation, security authentication, post-quantum cryptographic session management, and BetaChain access.

  • Session authentication tokens
  • CSRF protection tokens
  • Post-quantum key exchange state
  • BetaChain node routing preferences

2. Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences, language settings, dashboard configurations, and wallet display options. These enhance your experience but are not required for core functionality.

  • Language and region preferences
  • Dashboard layout and widget positions
  • Dark/light mode selection
  • Recently viewed trading pairs

3. Analytics Cookies

Help us understand platform usage, identify improvement areas, and monitor BetaChain performance. Data is aggregated and anonymized where possible.

  • Page views and navigation paths
  • Feature usage frequency
  • BetaChain latency metrics
  • Error and crash reporting

4. Marketing Cookies

Deliver relevant communications about new App Store releases, Insurance products, or Commerce features. Only used with explicit consent.

  • New feature announcements
  • BetaChain expansion updates
  • Developer program promotions
  • Partnership and integration news

Section 04

Your Privacy Choices

You have full control over your cookie preferences and tracking choices.

Cookie Consent Center

Manage cookie preferences through our Privacy Choices Center accessible from the website footer. Options include Accept All, Reject Non-Essential, or Customize by Category.

Do Not Track

We honor browser "Do Not Track" signals where technically feasible. However, necessary security and blockchain operation data collection continues regardless of this signal.

Third-Party Opt-Out

Where third-party analytics are used, you may opt-out through their respective mechanisms linked in our Privacy Choices Center.

Blockchain & Decentralized Storage

Unlike traditional cookies, some preferences may be stored in encrypted form or referenced via your wallet connection. Blockchain interactions do not use cookies but create immutable on-chain records.

Section 05

Managing Cookies by Browser

Desktop Browsers

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage cookies

Mobile Browsers

  • iOS Safari: Settings → Safari → Block All Cookies
  • Chrome (iOS): Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data
  • Chrome (Android): Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
  • Samsung Internet: Settings → Privacy → Delete browsing data
Privacy Choices Center

Manage Your Preferences

Open the Privacy Choices Center to review and update your cookie consent, tracking preferences, and data sharing settings at any time.
