GOOD TO KNOW

Pay-as-you-go, if you're not ready
Every account can transact without a subscription, at simple pay-as-you-go rates with no monthly commitment. It's the easiest way to put real value through Shamwari before choosing a tier.
  • Currency transfers: 0.75% (min $0.05, max $15)
  • Merchant payments: 0.50% (min $0.05, max $10)
  • Deposits to savings: always free
  • Full retail rate card and use cases on the Individuals page
How fair-use limits work
Every subscription tier includes a fair-use allowance. Here's what happens as you approach it.
  • Limits apply to both transaction count and monthly value — whichever you reach first
  • New subscribers get a one-month grace period at 120% of their limit
  • Unused capacity doesn't roll over, but annual subscribers get 10% higher limits at no extra cost
  • Go over your limit and you're simply billed at the overage rate — never cut off without warning
